Britney Manson

The internet sensation, model, singer and influencer Britney Manson became an iconic superstar overnight. Her first video "Walking till I get noticed by a model agency" took the fashion world by STORM and gained 40m views on Tiktok and an insane number of comments. She has now worked for famous names such as Valentino and Etro and has featured in Berlin and Milan Fashion weeks for the AW24 Season. Britney is a proud, trans woman who advocates for tras rights on her social media. She started her transition in middle school and has subsequently had a hard time in life. She has been bullied and beaten but because of this she became galvanized and rose from the ashes like a phoenix.
